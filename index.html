Akatsuki

The Akatsuki are major antagonists in the manga/anime series Naruto. They are a secret criminal organization that consists of powerful and dangerous rogue ninjas. they were a group of shinobi that existed outside the usual system of hidden villages. Over the course of several decades, Akatsuki took different forms and was led by different individuals. Though each iteration is viewed as either subversives or criminals, all seek to make the world a better place through their own means.
